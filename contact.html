Frequently Asked Questions

            

                

                    
How quickly can you start my project?

                    
We can typically start within 1-2 weeks, depending on project complexity and our current workload. For urgent projects, we offer expedited onboarding.

                

                

                    
Do you work with international clients?

                    
Absolutely! We work with clients worldwide and have experience with different time zones, languages, and cultural contexts. Our global presence ensures smooth collaboration.

                

                

                    
What is your typical project timeline?

                    
Project timelines vary based on scope and complexity. Simple websites take 2-4 weeks, while complex applications may take 3-6 months. We'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation.

                

                

                    
Do you provide ongoing maintenance?

                    
Yes! We offer comprehensive maintenance packages including bug fixes, security updates, performance optimization, and feature enhancements. We also provide 24/7 monitoring and support.

                

                

                    
How do you handle project communication?

                    
We use multiple communication channels including email, WhatsApp, video calls, and project management tools. You'll have a dedicated project manager and direct access to our development team.

                

                

                    
What if I'm not satisfied with the work?

                    
Your satisfaction is our priority. We offer unlimited revisions during development and a 30-day satisfaction guarantee post-launch. We work closely with you to ensure the final product exceeds your expectations.
